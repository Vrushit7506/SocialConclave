This is about us

Social Conclave is a one of its kind national conference wherein the delegates from across the nation will gather to discuss about the various social and economic concerns plaguing our country. The main aim of the conference is to find feasible solutions for the problems which are implemented by Team Social Impact. Multiple panel discussions will be held throughout the span of the conference and thus, the delegates would get a helping hand from the renowned panellists. The main objective of Social Conclave is to indulge the delegates in several inspiring discussions and debate sessions, with the hope of changing the face of India for the better. We will also be organising a field visit for the delegates to help them get to the crux of the situation.
